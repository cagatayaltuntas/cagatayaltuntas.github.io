An exploration of how we can use Python-based animations to teach complex number theory concepts to undergraduate students.

<!-- more -->

P-adic valuations represent a different way to measure the "size" of numbers, central to modern number theory. Teaching these concepts can often be difficult when restricted to a traditional chalkboard. 

With tools like Manim, we can create accurate, programmatic animations that allow students to visually comprehend these abstract mappings. In this post, I will explore a few simple functions that can get you started combining Python and Number Theory in the classroom.
